PROGRAM OVERVIEW

The Pillar of Patient Safety

Pharmacovigilance is the science and activities relating to the detection, assessment, understanding, and prevention of adverse effects. As regulatory requirements evolve, structured professional training is essential for those seeking to safeguard patient health and navigate industry standards.

In today's global pharmaceutical landscape, safety monitoring has moved from a reactive process to a proactive, data-driven discipline. This course prepares you for high-impact roles in safety operations and regulatory affairs.
