PROGRAM OVERVIEW

The Future of Value-Based Healthcare

Health Economics and Outcomes Research (HEOR) is a critical field evaluating the economic and clinical value of healthcare interventions. As healthcare costs rise, the demand for cost-effective therapies and evidence-based decision-making is at an all-time high.

This eCourse provides participants with foundational and applied knowledge in health economic evaluation, HTA frameworks, and market access strategies, preparing you for high-impact roles in the pharmaceutical and public health sectors.
